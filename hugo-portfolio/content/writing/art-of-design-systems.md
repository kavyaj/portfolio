# The Art of Design Systems

Design systems have become the backbone of modern product development. But what makes a design system truly effective?

## More Than Components

A good design system is:
- **Consistent**: Visual and interaction patterns that users can predict
- **Scalable**: Components that work across different contexts
- **Accessible**: Built with inclusivity in mind from the start
- **Living**: Continuously evolving based on team needs

## Building for the Future

The best design systems I've worked with treat documentation as a first-class citizen. When developers can understand the 'why' behind design decisions, magic happens.

## Key Takeaways

1. Start small and grow organically
2. Invest in tooling and automation
3. Make adoption easy for your team
4. Measure success through usage, not just completion
