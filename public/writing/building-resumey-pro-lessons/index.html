Building Resumey.Pro: Lessons from My First SaaS

After quitting my job to build a resume builder, here are the key lessons I learned about product development, user feedback, and entrepreneurship.

Building Resumey.Pro: Lessons from My First SaaS

After spending years in traditional design roles, I made the leap to build my own product. Resumey.Pro started as a simple idea: what if creating a professional resume could be as easy as filling out a form?

The Journey

The development process taught me several key lessons:

1. Start with the Problem

I spent too much time on features nobody asked for. The real breakthrough came when I started talking to users daily.

2. Design Systems Matter

Building a consistent design system from day one saved countless hours later in development.

3. User Feedback is Gold

Every piece of feedback, positive or negative, helped shape the product into something people actually wanted to use.

What’s Next

Building Resumey.Pro was just the beginning. The experience has opened my eyes to the possibilities of combining design thinking with entrepreneurial spirit.
